iTrade Track Sales ("the App") is developed and managed by INP Organization. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users.

The app only uses details provided by the user within the app for the purpose of:

  • Attendance marking
  • Bid submission

  • Feedback submission

No additional data is collected by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide inside the app is:

  • Stored securely on our backend server powered by Supabase
  • Accessible only to your organization's authorized admin personnel

We do not use your information for advertising, promotions, or any third-party marketing.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is not sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

Organization admin can view the data you submit, such as attendance logs, bid details, or feedback, for internal operational use only.

4. Data Security

We maintain strict security measures to protect your data. All data transfers are encrypted, and sensitive data is accessible only to authorized personnel.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or modify any information you submit through the app
  • Withdraw permissions (like location access) from your device settings anytime

Since no additional personal data is collected, no further data rights management is required.

We may update this Privacy Policy if required. Updates will be posted within the app and on our official website.
